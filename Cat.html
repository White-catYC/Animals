Breed black-footed cat(small-spotted cat)
Head-and-body length 35-52 cm(14-20 in)
Life Span Max about 15 years
Origin Northern Karoo of South Africa
Size Smallest cat species in Africa
Temperament Extreme unsocial
Coat Tawny fur that is entirely covered with black spots
Behavior Nocturnal and usually solitary, except when females care for dependent kittens
Hunting and diet Mainly small prey such as rodents and small birds
